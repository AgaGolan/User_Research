Journey map - Natalia

User persona

Natalia - Technical Communication Student/Office Worker

User goal

Natalia’s goal is to make a COVID-19 vaccination appointment at a convenient date, in the afternoon and at a place close to her workplace.

Scenario

Natalia goes to https://pacjent.gov.pl/ to find a suitable date and place for her vaccination.

User backstory

Natalia shares her time between work-home and school that's why:

  • she doesn't want to make calls and visit places to register;

  • she wants to get vaccinated on her way back from work;

  • she hopes that the registration process will be quick and simple and that she will receive a confirmation of registration;

  • she has already used IKP so she expects to find information about registration for vaccination on it.

Journey map

Stage

Finding the website

Entering the website

Login process

Starting the registration process

Making an appointment for vaccination

Getting confirmation

User action

Goes to https://pacjent.gov.pl/ using a PC and searches for it using “IKP” as key word.

Checks the information on the page that appears.

Clicks the “Zaloguj się” button in the top right.

Clicks “Zaloguj się do e-rejestracji.” to make a quick appointment for COVID-19 vaccination and chooses to log in through a trusted profile “Profil Zaufany”.

After successfully logging in, Natalia is redirected to the vaccination registration page.

Step not reached.

Pain points or questions

Key word “IKP” took her to the appropriate page (home/login). It was easy to find - as the 2nd result in the search engine and easily described.

A lot of information and parts of website.

Another 2 login buttons will appears.

Another log in button appears…

but in the next step user can select the authentication/login method.

Here user can see previous vaccinations (completely unnecessary - user can check it on the patient's account if necessary in another tab, and this is the first thing that is displayed. The user can edit her data and see her vaccination referrals.

Natalia did not receive confirmation because she did not register for vaccination. The goal has not been achieved.

Emotions

Opportunities and solutions

  • The 1st result is about opportunities that IKP gives its users, but there should be Home page of IKP.

  • This causes the user to unnecessarily make sure which page she clicks on and which service it is connected to.

Due to the nature of the website, it contains a lot of information, but it’s arranged in a logical and easy-to-navigate way.

On the homepage there are min. 2 login buttons. One would be enough, because two give the feeling that the user can log in to several places.

In this step the user has similar situation like in the previous one. It's confusing.

It could be done in fewer steps, e.g. Log in to IKP and enter your login and password, and then register for vaccination.

The registration page turned out to be the end of the user's journey. Due to the fact that she did not have an e-referral, she could not register for vaccination with a booster dose.

At this point, the user is not informed about solving the problem (e.g. a button that would concern issuing an e-referral)

Step not reached.
